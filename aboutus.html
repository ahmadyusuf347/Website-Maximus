About Us


PT Maximus Technology Indonesia is established by a group of professionals that accumulate experience on ICT projects in the Government sector in Indonesia for more than 20 years.

Such experience includes flagship projects that have boosted the digitalization of government services in different sectors in Indonesia (Immigration, Land Registration, Civil Service, Foreign Affairs, Smart Cities, Taxation, Think Tanks, Education, Healthcare, etc.)

Maximus was born to become the platform where we can work and deliver our professional services the way we have always believed in, with passion and ensuring the most efficient and advanced solutions for our customers.
